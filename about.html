Why Use This Tool?

This word counter tool is a free and easy-to-use web app for writers, students, bloggers, and professionals. It allows you to quickly analyze your text by providing:

  • ✔️ Word count
  • ✔️ Character count
  • ✔️ Estimated reading time

Who Can Use It?

Whether you're preparing a college essay, a blog post, or a professional document, this tool ensures you stay within word limits and better understand your writing length.
